Rules

  1. The wheel has 12 segments with different cash values, prizes, penalties and others.
  2. Players spin the wheel and depending on the segment they are either paid out and get the chance to pick a letter or get a penalty.
  3. If the segment is not a penalty one, the player can pick one consonant.
  4. If the guess is correct, then a prize according to the segment is paid.
  5. If the guess is wrong, the next player takes the turn.
