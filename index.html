Rules

  1. On the game screen, the player is presented with a category, and a hidden puzzle associated with that category.
  2. A random monetary value is presented for each letter guess that they make. This amount could be a dollar amount, but it could also be "Bankrupt", where you lose everything.
  3. If the segment is not a penalty one, the player can pick a letter.
  4. If the guess is correct, then a prize according to the segment is paid, per correct letter ($100 for 3 x J's would pay out $300).
  5. If the guess is wrong, a new prize amount is generated, and the player gets to guess again.
  6. After solving the puzzle, the player moves on to the next round. There are a total of 3 rounds.
  7. At the completion of all 3 rounds, the players total winnings are added up, and if achieved, the player is immortalized on the High Score Board
